Behind the project - The idea

My family owns a very small business. It is a traditional sicilian coffee shop, where you can drink your coffee and taste typical sicilian food, mainly from Messina. Since it is a very small and closed business, we didn't feel the urge to do something that professional as a website, this until now. I realized that a well-structured website isn't just a professional requirement, it's a powerful tool to share our story and reach a wider audience.
